About Us

Jagannath Bastralaya is a family-run tailoring and uniform supply business known for quality, durability, and honest pricing. Our work is founded on craftsmanship and trust passed down through generations.

Our Story

Founded by Bholanath Saha in 1957, the business grew from a small tailoring workshop to a trusted local supplier for schools and institutions in Dumdum and greater Kolkata. The family continues the tradition of careful fabric selection, accurate stitching, and timely delivery.

Our Mission

To deliver durable, comfortable uniforms at fair prices, ensuring institutions receive consistent, well-fitted garments with prompt service.
